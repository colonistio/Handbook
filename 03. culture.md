# Culture

## All-Remote
+ We are fully remote, flexible schedule.
+ Full-time Guild members are expected to deliver an output of 40 hours/week.

## Self Managing
+ No traditional managers. 
+ Company sets goals, everyone executes independently.
+ Manage your own time and unblock yourself.

## Product Ownership
+ You have full ownership of your tasks from start to finish.
+ Try to do your job in the best way possible for the product, not to please anyone.

## Dependability
+ Work on your tasks, the team depends on every one of them.
+ Join meetings on time.
+ Meet deadlines.
+ Leave daily/weekly updates on time.
+ Follow team processes.

## Efficiency
+ Execute your tasks step by step. Each step should be a workable state.
+ Try to resolve a problem yourself for a bit. If unsuccessful, ask for help.

### Common ways to resolve a problem
* Check documentation. We regularly put our experience in writing.
* Search Discord. Someone probably already asked the same thing.
* Search Google, ask AI.
* Anticipate needs and ask before starting your task.

## Accountability

+ No one is socially or career-punished for mistakes.
+ Problems identify processes flaw, not someone's failures.
+ We speak openly about mistakes, try to resolve and prevent them in future.

## Learning

+ Learn parts of the product that you don’t know.
+ Make sure >10% of your work is challenging for you.

## Fun
+ We are here to create fun for players and ourselves.
+ Everyone’s individual role should be enjoyable and somewhere they shine.
+ We enjoy playing games and hanging out together. Participation is always optional.
