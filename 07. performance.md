# Growth & Performance

## Biannual Cycles
+ Performance review happens twice a year.

+ The process for each team member is as follows:
  1. Get/provide peer feedback
  2. Reflect on your impact
  3. Come up with a plan to increase your impact
  4. Discuss with your manager

## Beyond Cycles

+ You are encouraged to ask for a feedback frequently from 
  + Peers
  + Your manager
  + Anyone impacted by your work
  
+ More on the [feedback communication](https://github.com/colonistio/Handbook/blob/main/04.%20communication.md#feedback)
